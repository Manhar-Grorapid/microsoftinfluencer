Microsoft 365 SMB Masters Program

Learn how to maximize the analytics opportunity with Microsoft with this 4-part webinar series

Organizations are eager to harness their data to gain a competitive edge. This presents a massive opportunity to partners. So much so that according to Forrester, 40% of the Azure projects include an analytics component in Year 1, which grows by 35% YoY. In this 4-part digital series, we will help you build and scale strong GTM practices, aligned to the Microsoft Customer Engagement Methodology (MCEM).

We will cover how you can:

  1. Maximize partner growth and profitability through investments and incentives utilization across the MCEM (CiaB, Workshop, Azure Innovate, Deployment, and WANI)
  2. Enhance sales and marketing performance through clarity on use cases, value proposition, and ITDM and BDM messaging
  3. Improve co-selling outcomes by landing your differentiated value proposition and aligning with Microsoft messaging and customer engagement methodology

Session 1 | Analytics Opportunity and GTM Readiness

In the first part of the series, we will:

  1. Evaluate the analytics opportunity with Microsoft Analytics Solution, including Microsoft Fabric
  2. Discuss the key customer scenarios and learn how to identify new opportunities for different workloads
  3. Discuss how to win by leading with a business outcome centric message.
  4. Share the key customer call to actions and Microsoft GTM resources across the MCEM, including industry dream demos and campaign in a box (CiaB)
