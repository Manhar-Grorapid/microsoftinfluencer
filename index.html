Microsoft 365 SMB Masters Program

Unlock the potential of Microsoft 365 with security managed services 3-part webinar series

Managed services are a key partner profitability and revenue growth driver, and security services represent the largest opportunity for partners. In this 3-part digital series, we will help partners capitalize on this opportunity by transforming security offerings with the new security managed services partner kit.

The first part of the series focuses on the business development and sales perspective, followed by two technical sessions. The first technical session is built for partners looking to start with security services and Business Premium. The second technical session is focused on expanding to more advanced security services, intended for partners already selling and providing services with M365 Business Premium.

Part 1 | Driving growth with security managed services

In this first of this three-part webinar series: partner business decision-makers will:

  • Understand the Microsoft 365 profitability and revenue growth opportunities with security managed services
  • Understand the key aspects of developing successful security offerings for SMB customers
  • Understand how to adopt the new managed security services partner kit to begin, or further develop security offerings with managed services

  • Walking through the key steps needed to secure customers environments with M365 Business Premium
  • Demo’s showing best practices and tips on how to get started with managing the key security features in M365 Business Premium

  • Learn how to leverage customer feedback and analytics to drive continuous improvement and innovation in your security services
